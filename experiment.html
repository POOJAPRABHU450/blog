About Me

My Childhood

I had a childhood where my dad showed me “How beautiful the world is”,but my Mom made sure to realise me “What the real world is”.I had a childhood where my dad taught me self-esteem but there was my mom who taught me the sacrifice to keep a family together.The night I was struggling for life as a toddler but my mom had hope and her prayers made miracle which not a single doctor believed. I think my childhood was PERFECT.
